In the SBML subset of MathML~2.0, the MathML attribute
\token{definitionURL} is only permitted on \token{ci}, \token{csymbol},
and \token{semantics}.  No other
MathML elements may have a \token{definitionURL} attribute.  (References:
L2V2 Section~3.5.1; L2V3 Section~3.4.1; L2V4 Section~3.4.1; L2V5 Section~\ref{sec:mathmlsubset}.)
