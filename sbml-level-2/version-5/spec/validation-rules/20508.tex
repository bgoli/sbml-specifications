The value of the \token{units} attribute on a \Compartment having
\token{spatialDimensions} of \val{2} must be either \val{area},
\val{dimensionless}, or the identifier of a \UnitDefinition based on
either \token{metre} (with \token{exponent} equal to \val{2}) or
\token{dimensionless}.  (References: L2V1 Section 4.5.4; L2V2
Section~4.7.5; L2V3 Section~4.7.5; L2V4 Section~4.7.5; L2V5 Section~\ref{sec:compartment-units}.)
