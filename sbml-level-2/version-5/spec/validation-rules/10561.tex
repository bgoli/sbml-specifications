When the \token{variable} attribute of an \EventAssignment
contains the identifier of a \Compartment in the model, the
units of the mathematical expression in the
\EventAssignment's \token{math} expression should be
consistent with the units of that compartment's size.
(References: L2V2 Section~4.14.2; L2V3 Section~4.14.4; L2V4 Section~4.14.4\changed{; L2V5 Section~\ref{sec:eventassignment}}.)
