values (that is, MathML real, integer or ``e-notation'' numbers,
or the time or delay \token{csymbol}): \token{math} in \KineticLaw,
\token{stoichi\-ometryMath} in \SpeciesReference, \token{math} in
\InitialAssignment, \token{math} in \AssignmentRule, \token{math} in
\RateRule, \token{math} in \AlgebraicRule, \token{math} in \Event
\Delay, and \token{math} in \EventAssignment.  (References: L2V2
Sections~4.10, 4.11, 4.13 and~4.14; L3V3 Sections~4.10, 4.11, 4.13 
and~4.14; L2V4 Sections~4.10, 4.11, 4.13, and~4.14; L2V5 Sections~\ref{sec:initialAssignment}, \ref{sec:rules},
