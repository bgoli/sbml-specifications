The \token{listOf\_\_\_} containers in a \Model are optional, but if
present, the lists cannot be empty.  Specifically, if any of the
following are present in a \Model, they must not be empty:
\token{listOfFunctionDefinitions}, \token{listOfUnitDefinitions},
\token{listOfCompartmentTypes}, \token{listOfSpeciesTypes},
\token{listOfCompartments}, \token{listOfSpecies},
\token{listOfParameters}, \token{listOfInitialAssignments},
\token{listOfRules}, \token{listOfConstraints},
\token{listOfReactions} and \token{listOfEvents}.
