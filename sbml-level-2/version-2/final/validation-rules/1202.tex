Redefinitions of the built-in unit \token{substance} must be based on the
units \token{mole}, \token{item}, \token{gram}, \token{kilogram}, or
\token{dimensionless}.  More formally, a \UnitDefinition for
\token{substance} must simplify to a single \Unit whose \token{kind} field
has a value of \val{mole}, \val{item}, \val{gram}, \val{kilogram}, or
\token{dimensionless}, and whose \token{exponent} field has a value of
\val{1}.  (References: L2V1 Section~4.4.3; L2V2
Section~\ref{sec:built-in-units}.)
