Redefinitions of the built-in unit \token{volume} must be based on
\token{litre}, \token{metre} or \token{dimensionless}.  More formally, a
\UnitDefinition for \token{volume} must simplify to a single \Unit in which
the \token{kind} field value is either \val{litre}, \val{metre}, or
\val{dimensionless}.  Additional constraints apply if the kind is
\val{litre} or \val{metre}.  (References: L2V1 Section 4.4.3; L2V2
Section~\ref{sec:built-in-units}.)
