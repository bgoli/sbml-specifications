The value of an \Event's \token{timeUnits} must be \val{time},
\val{second}, \val{dimensionless}, or the identifier of a
\UnitDefinition derived from either \token{second} (with an
\token{exponent} value of \val{1}) or \val{dimensionless}.
(References: L2V1 Section 4.10.4; L2V2 Section~\ref{sec:event-timeunits}.)
