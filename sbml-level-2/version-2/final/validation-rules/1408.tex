The value of a \Species's \token{substanceUnits} field must be either
\val{substance}, \val{mole}, \val{kilogram}, \val{item},
\val{dimensionless}, or the identifier of a \UnitDefinition derived from
\val{mole} (with an \token{exponent} of \val{1}), \val{kilogram}
(with an \token{exponent} of \val{1}), \val{item} (with an
\token{exponent} of \val{1}), or \val{dimensionless}.  (References:
L2V1 Section 4.6.4; L2V2 Section~\ref{sec:species-units}.)
