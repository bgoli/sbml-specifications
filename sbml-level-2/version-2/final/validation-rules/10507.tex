The value of the \token{units} field on a \Compartment having
\token{spatialDimensions} of \val{1} must be either \val{length},
\val{metre}, \val{dimensionless}, or the identifier of a
\UnitDefinition based on either \token{metre} (with \token{exponent} equal
to \val{1}) or \token{dimensionless}.  (References: L2V1 Section 4.5.4;
L2V2 Section~\ref{sec:compartment-units}.)
