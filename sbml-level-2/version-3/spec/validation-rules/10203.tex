In the SBML subset of MathML~2.0, the MathML attribute
\token{encoding} is only permitted on \token{csymbol}\changed{, 
\token{annotation} and \token{annotation-xml}}.  No other
MathML elements may have the \token{encoding} attribute.
(References: L2V2 Section~3.5.1; L2V3 Section~\ref{sec:mathmlsubset}.)
