The only permitted MathML~2.0 elements in SBML Level~2 are
the following: \token{cn}, \token{ci}, \token{csymbol},
\token{sep}, \token{apply}, \token{piecewise}, \token{piece},
\token{otherwise}, \token{eq}, \token{neq}, \token{gt},
\token{lt}, \token{geq}, \token{leq}, \token{plus}, \token{minus},
\token{times}, \token{divide}, \token{power}, \token{root},
\token{abs}, \token{exp}, \token{ln}, \token{log}, \token{floor},
\token{ceiling}, \token{factorial}, \token{and}, \token{or},
\token{xor}, \token{not}, \token{degree}, \token{bvar},
\token{logbase}, \token{sin}, \token{cos}, \token{tan},
\token{sec}, \token{csc}, \token{cot}, \token{sinh}, \token{cosh},
\token{tanh}, \token{sech}, \token{csch}, \token{coth},
\token{arcsin}, \token{arccos}, \token{arctan}, \token{arcsec},
\token{arccsc}, \token{arccot}, \token{arcsinh}, \token{arccosh},
\token{arctanh}, \token{arcsech}, \token{arccsch},
\token{arccoth}, \token{true}, \token{false}, \token{notanumber},
\token{pi}, \token{infinity}, \token{exponentiale},
\token{semantics}, \token{annotation}, and \token{annotation-xml}.
(References: L2V2 Section~3.5.1; L2V3 Section~\ref{sec:mathmlsubset}.)
