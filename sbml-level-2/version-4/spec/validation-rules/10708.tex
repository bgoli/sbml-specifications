The value of the \token{sboTerm} attribute on a \SpeciesReference or a
\ModifierSpeciesReference \changed{should} be an SBO identifier referring to a
\changed{participant role}.  That is, the value \changed{should} be a term derived
from \sboparticipantrole.  \changed{The appropriate term depends on whether
the entity is} \changed{a reactant, product or modifier.}  (References: 
L2V2 Section~4.13.2; L2V3 Sections~4.13.1 and~5; L2V4 Sections~\ref{sec:reaction-sboterm} and~\ref{sec:sbo}.)
