In a \KineticLaw object, the unit of measurement associated with the
formula in the \KineticLaw's \token{math} expression should be equal to
\{unit of reaction \emph{extent}\}/\{unit of \emph{time}\}.  (References:
SBML L3V2 \sec{sec:constructing-equations}, \sec{sec:timeunits}
and~\sec{sec:raterule}.)
