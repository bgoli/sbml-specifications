In a \KineticLaw object, the unit of measurement associated with the
formula in the \KineticLaw's \token{math} expression should be equal to
\{unit of reaction \emph{extent}\}/\{unit of \emph{time}\}.  (References: \tworefs{4.11.7, 4.2.4, and 4.9.4}{\ref{sec:constructing-equations}, \ref{sec:timeunits}, and~\ref{sec:raterule}}.)
