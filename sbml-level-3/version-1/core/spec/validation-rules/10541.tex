The units of the \token{math} formula in a \KineticLaw definition should be
equivalent to the units of \quantity{extent per time}, where the units of
\quantity{extent} are determined by the \Model's \token{extentUnits}
attribute and \quantity{time} by the \Model's \token{timeUnits} attribute.
(References: L2V2 Section~4.13.5; L2V3 Section~4.13.5; L2V4 Section~4.13.5;
L3V1 Section~\ref{subsec:kinetic-law}.)
