The MathML formulas in the following elements must yield numeric
values (that is, MathML real, integer or ``e-notation'' numbers,
or the \emph{time}, \emph{delay} or \emph{avogadro} \token{csymbol}):
\token{math} in \KineticLaw, \token{math} in
\InitialAssignment, \token{math} in \AssignmentRule, \token{math} in
\RateRule, \token{math} in \AlgebraicRule, \token{math} in \Event
\Delay, and \token{math} in \EventAssignment.  (References: SBML L3V1 
Sections~\ref{sec:initialAssignment}, \ref{sec:rules}, 
\ref{sec:reactions} and~\ref{sec:events}.)
